2. Fly Out Components

Click the buttons below to open fly-outs from all four directions. Features include focus trapping, ESC to close, click outside to close, and scroll locking.

Features

  • Smooth scroll animations with requestAnimationFrame
  • Works with both window and container scroll
  • Accessibility features: focus trap, ARIA attributes, keyboard support
  • Customizable positions and offsets
  • Lightweight and dependency-free
